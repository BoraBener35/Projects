Why Yeditepe

Yeditepe University, which continues its academic activities in a wide range of fields from Science to Social Sciences, from Fine Arts to Health Sciences and Educational Sciences, offers its students double major, minor and overseas education opportunities and brings them together with an education that is suitable for the requirements of the developing world. In addition to its academic activities, it provides the opportunity for students to develop themselves with 44 student clubs and activities in the campus environment. With its achievements in 42 sports branches in which it operates in the field of sports, it ranks among the top universities every year. Yeditepe University; It leads higher education in Turkey by providing more than 700 collaborations with the world's leading universities, especially in Europe, the USA, Canada, Russia, South America and the Far East. Yeditepe University, which holds the flag as the university that exchanges the most foreign students with Erasmus and Exchange among foundation universities; With the Academic Cooperation Agreement it has made with Houston Methodist Global, which includes the Faculties of Pharmacy, Health Sciences, Medicine, Dentistry and Business, it expands the steps it has taken in this direction. "University-Industry Collaboration" projects, which we successfully carry out and which we believe are extremely necessary for students, are also among our priorities. Yeditepe University Automotive Club, which includes students from various departments of our university, continues to work actively. Our club, which was entitled to participate in the TÜBİTAK Efficiency Challenge electric vehicle competition in 2018, continues to work on the design and production of the vehicle. In addition, our Yeditepe University Shell Eco-Marathon team, which has been participating regularly in the Shell Eco-Marathon competition since 2010, will represent us in the Electric Vehicle category in the competition to be held in London this year and in which 3000 students from 200 teams will participate, with the support of our university and companies. Yeditepe University established Yeditepe Sağlik Hizmetleri A.Ş. in 2005 to bring together academic innovations with the marketing expertise of a dynamic company. Our company, which has brought many care products to the market over the years, has released organic fertilizers, which it has produced with the philosophy of Innovative Agriculture, to the market with the brand Bactogen in order to raise healthier generations and to eliminate the factors that negatively affect human health. Bactogen products get their strength from the expert staff of Yeditepe University Genetics and Bioengineering Department and laboratories equipped with the latest technology.
